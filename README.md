**98% / 2%: the ninety-eight and my two percent**
——— *an interactive audiovisual piece for distributed performance*

> Most philosophers and psychologists would encourage us to accept that our lives are largely beyond our control. I take this as a given — call it 98%. 
The more I internalize this, the more I find myself holding onto the remaining 2%. 

This piece is a direct expression of that obsession. It is also an experiment in what distributed performance can feel like — two or more people, in different locations, opening the same page, listening to the same waves, each deciding when to push back. The progress bar descends regardless. The 2% remains.

This is an ongoing project. The rules are not fixed. The sound is not finished. What it can become is still open.
